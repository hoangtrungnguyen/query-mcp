## Tips for Getting Better Answers

**Be specific about time.** "Last month" works. So does "Q1 2025" or "the past 90 days."

**Name the metric.** "Top customers by revenue" is clearer than "top customers."

**Ask for comparisons.** "This month vs. last month" or "year-over-year" gives you context.

**Request ranking.** "Top 10 by X" or "lowest 5 by Y" focuses the result.

**Ask follow-up questions.** After any answer, you can ask for more detail, a different angle, or a breakout by segment.
